Editorial Board

Dr. Waleed Ziad

Associate Professor, Religious Studies

Renowned historian of Afghanistan, Central & South Asia, and the Persianate world. Though unable to actively participate due to his busy academic schedule, he graciously serves on our Editorial Board with a special focus on manuscript preservation and archival research.
