Patron-in-Chief

Pir Mufti Khair Muhammad Bhorvi

Pir Mufti Khair Muhammad Bhorvi is the Patron-in-Chief of Darbar-e-Aalia Naqshbandia Mujaddadia Bhor Sharif (Mianwali) and a distinguished author of numerous scholarly works. His guidance anchors the organization in traditional Sufi values while actively supporting contemporary intellectual pursuits in Islamic thought, interfaith dialogue, and the promotion of peace and harmony. Under his spiritual leadership, Khair Publications continues to bridge classical spirituality with modern scholarship, fostering an inclusive and enlightened approach to religious understanding.
